routines and earn points as you complete them.

leaderboard. Social pressure, turned into a game.

🥑 REACH A DIETITIAN
Browse expert dietitians, book a session, ask your questions.

Social Fit is a motivation and tracking tool for healthy living; it is not
a substitute for medical advice. Discuss nutrition choices with an expert.

Download free and start with your first micro-task today. 🌿
